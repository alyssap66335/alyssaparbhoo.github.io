Breakfast

Overnight Oats

    Ingredients:
  • 100g rolled oats
  • 100g whole milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 oz berries
    Directions:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a jar.
  2. Refrigerate overnight.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
